How modern machine learning approaches can be used as diagnosis tools

It is no secret that machine learning is one of the most powerful tools nowadays, allowing us to extract important information from a large dataset. Alerta extracts factors determining your probability of suffering from endometriosis with 93% sensitivity and 99% specificity.
